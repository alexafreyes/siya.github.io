RECLAIM YOUR ROLE

The Philippines has been praised as one fo the most accepting countries to the LGBT+ community and champions its inclusion of queer talent. However, while Filipino media is promoting many transgender artists, it still continues to occasionally fail at prime opportunities of inclusion.

We intend to highlight these shortcomings from recent Pinoy films that tell transgender stories but cast cisgender actors by placing potential transgender actors directly into the roles, therefore as a result, reclaiming them and putting the stories in their rightful places in queer storytelling.
